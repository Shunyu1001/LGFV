government financing functions and transformed into a marketized
self-operating entity. The same packet states that the issuer does not
undertake government financing functions after January 1, 2015 and that new

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because formal marketization and
no-government-financing language coexists with continuing infrastructure,
land-related, fiscal-support, and public-service functions inside the same
issuer. The alternative label is functional transfer, but the evidence does
not show that the public-development function moved outside the issuer.

## Caveat

The formal event is supported by issuer and legal-opinion disclosures rather
than an independently collected platform-list exit document. Two documents in
the packet were not machine-readable.
