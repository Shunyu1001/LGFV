# Shangrao Innovation Development Industrial Investment Group

Case ID: `sch_20260630_0055`

Company: 上饶创新发展产业投资集团有限公司

Final label: `nominal_exit`

Confidence: `medium`

## Formal event

The prospectus states that the issuer's debt will be repaid by the issuer as an
independent legal person, that the local state acts only as shareholder with
limited liability, and that the company does not undertake government financing
functions. It also states that new debt after January 1, 2015 is not
local-government debt. The legal opinion repeats the no-hidden-debt and
no-fiscal-repayment commitments for the current MTN.

The same source packet describes the issuer as the largest state-owned holding
company in Shangrao Economic and Technological Development Zone and as the
zone's main infrastructure construction and investment-financing body. It
reports entrusted construction agreements with the development-zone management
committee, PPP projects, government purchase service projects, fiscal budget
arrangements, receivables from the development-zone management committee, and
continuing fiscal subsidies. The 2024 financial statements also show large
infrastructure-construction agency revenue, government subsidies, and
subsidiaries whose business nature is infrastructure construction.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit. Formal compliance language coexists with a
continuing role in infrastructure construction, agency construction, PPP
projects, government purchase service, development-zone fiscal payments,
government receivables, and fiscal subsidies. The issuer therefore fits the
paper's central category in which official exit does not amount to functional
exit.

## Caveat

This is a development-zone platform rather than a generic prefecture-level
urban construction company. It remains in scope because the issuer is a local
state-owned investment and infrastructure vehicle with documented
public-development and financing functions. The formal event is based on
prospectus and legal-opinion compliance language rather than an independently
retrieved platform-list transfer-out notice.
