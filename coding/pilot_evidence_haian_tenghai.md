# Jiangsu Tenghai Investment Holding Group

Case ID: `sch_20260630_0019`

Company: 江苏腾海投资控股集团有限公司

Final label: `nominal_exit`

Confidence: `medium`

## Formal event

The 2026 MTN prospectus states that the issuer will not use bond proceeds in a
way that adds local-government debt or hidden debt, will not transfer proceeds
to government or fiscal departments, and will not rely on direct fiscal funds
for repayment. It also states that the shareholder bears only limited liability,
that the issuer is responsible for repayment as an independent legal person,
that the issuer does not undertake government financing functions, and that new
debt after January 1, 2015 is not local-government debt. The legal opinion
confirms that the issuer has no illegal government guarantee, no land-reserve
function, no government-credit-linked misleading publicity, no PPP, government
investment fund, BT, government-purchase-service, repurchase, or advance
financing arrangement, and that the current debt instrument will not increase
government debt or hidden debt.

## Continued function

The same packet shows continuing public-development functions inside the issuer
group. The prospectus describes Tenghai as one of Haian's local government
financing platforms and as the main investment and construction body for
infrastructure in the Haian Economic and Technological Development Zone. It
states that the local government supports the issuer through asset injection,
policy support, and fiscal subsidies. It records high government-related
receivables, heavy reliance on fiscal cash inflows from entrusted construction,
housing construction, subsidies, and government-related current accounts,
continuing resettlement-housing and infrastructure construction, and contracts
with local government departments. The issuer's own departments manage
resettlement housing and municipal engineering. Financial statements also show
large receivables from the development-zone management committee and related
state entities, special payables for wastewater treatment, industrial-park, and
resettlement-housing projects, and public development revenue through housing
construction.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit. The issuer's documents contain clean
post-2015 compliance language and legal opinions denying government financing,
but the same source packet shows that the issuer remains a development-zone
public-development platform. The core function is not generic commercial
holding. It is housing construction, infrastructure construction, municipal
engineering, government-related receivables, fiscal cash-flow dependence,
special project funds, and state-asset support. Formal legal separation does
not remove the local public-development function from the issuer group.

## Caveat

The case is a county-level development-zone platform under Haian rather than a
prefecture-level city investment group. The historical-capacity match therefore
uses Nantong as the contemporary prefecture-level unit. The formal event is
based on issuer disclosure and legal-opinion compliance language rather than a
separate administrative platform-list exit notice, so the label remains medium
confidence.
