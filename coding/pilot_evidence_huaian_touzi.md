## Formal event

The prospectus provides direct list-status evidence. It states that the issuer
entered the CBRC financing-platform list in 2010 and was transferred out of the
list on June 13, 2011. Later compliance language also states that the issuer
does not undertake government financing functions after January 1, 2015.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because direct formal exit-list evidence
coexists with continuing platform-like public-development functions inside the
same issuer. This is one of the stronger cases for the paper's distinction
between official exit and institutional exit.

## Caveat

The direct list evidence is a later prospectus disclosure rather than the
original 2011 regulator document. Confidence is nevertheless high because the
same source packet contains both direct formal-exit evidence and extensive
continued-function evidence.
