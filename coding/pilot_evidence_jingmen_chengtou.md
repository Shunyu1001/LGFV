## Formal event

The prospectus and legal opinion state that the issuer does not undertake
government financing functions after January 1, 2015. They also state that the
new debt does not constitute local-government debt or hidden debt and will not
be directly repaid by fiscal funds.

government purchase service, PPP-related investment, entrusted construction,

project backgrounds, which supports a nominal-exit classification rather than

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because compliance language coexists with
continuing public-development finance through the same issuer. The alternative
label is functional transfer or fiscal substitution, but the present evidence
does not show that the functions moved out of the issuer.

## Caveat

The current evidence comes from issuer and legal-opinion disclosures rather
than an original exit-list document. Some audit files were scanned and not
machine-readable in this pass.
