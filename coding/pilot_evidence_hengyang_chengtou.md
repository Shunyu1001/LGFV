The prospectus states that the issuer had been included in local government financing platform management after the 2010 clean-up, exited the list in September 2011, returned to platform management in March 2012 because it undertook affordable-housing projects, and formally transferred out of CBIRC platform management on February 25, 2020.

The issuer and its subsidiary signed entrusted-construction agreements with government-side principals. ## Coding Implication

The final label is `nominal_exit`. Formal exit is directly documented, but public project and fiscal-settlement functions continued inside the same issuer. The case should become the benchmark for distinguishing official platform-list exit from substantive institutional exit.
