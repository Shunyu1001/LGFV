## Boundary reason

This packet is useful for the paper but should not enter the gold-standard
city-platform label file. Zhejiang Provincial Communications Investment Group
is a province-level transportation SOE, not a municipal LGFV or city platform
in the current sampling frame. The documents contain strong evidence of
formalized infrastructure finance, public-asset operation, PPP arrangements,
government subsidies, and no-hidden-debt compliance language. They do not show
a direct official exit, transfer, list-removal, or marketization event for a
named city-level platform.

## Formal compliance evidence

The prospectus states that proceeds will repay interest-bearing debt and that
the new debt will not increase local government debt or hidden debt. It also
states that the local government will not directly repay the debt through
fiscal funds. The company describes itself as a provincial transport
state-owned asset operating institution and states that it does not undertake
government financing functions after January 1, 2015.

infrastructure finance and LGFV exit. It should discipline the codebook by
