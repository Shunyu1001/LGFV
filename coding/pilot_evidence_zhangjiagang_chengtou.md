## Coding Summary

Zhangjiagang City Investment is coded as nominal exit. The source packet
contains formal compliance language: the legal opinion states that the issuer
and its subsidiaries were not included in the 2018 CBIRC financing-platform
list, and that the current issue will not increase local-government debt or
hidden debt. Yet the same packet shows that the issuer remains deeply embedded
in land preparation, resettlement housing, infrastructure construction,
settlement with the land reserve center, fiscal support, and government-linked
receivables.

The institutional category has changed more clearly than the underlying
public-development function. This is not a clean substantive exit. It is also
not coded as functional transfer because the available evidence still places
the core development tasks inside the same issuer rather than clearly moving
them to a separate fiscal or state-asset vehicle.
