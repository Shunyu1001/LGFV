## Coding Summary

Lu'an City Construction Investment is coded as nominal exit. The issuer's
documents state that after the post-2012 policy shift it no longer participates
in BT projects and does not undertake government financing functions. At the
same time, the packet shows continuing entrusted construction, infrastructure,
affordable housing, PPP activity, fiscal payment, government receivables,
special construction payables, and fiscal subsidies.

The continued public-development function remains attached to the same issuer.
The case is therefore not substantive exit. A formal fiscal-substitution reading
is plausible, but the institutional relocation is not clear enough for
functional transfer.

## Ambiguity

The strongest alternative label is functional transfer because the issuer uses
compliant delegated-construction and fiscal-settlement language. The final
label remains nominal because project execution and public-development
financing still run through the same issuer.
