## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because formal stripping and marketized
transformation coexist with the same issuer continuing the central
public-project role. A fiscal-substitution reading is plausible because many
arrangements are described as compliant delegated construction or marketized
compensation, but the functional location remains inside the issuer.

## Caveat

The formal event is supported by issuer disclosures rather than an
independently collected exit-list document. Some attachments were scanned and
not machine-readable, but the prospectus and audit reports provide extensive
evidence.
