## Formal event

The prospectus states that the issuer does not undertake government financing
functions and that new debt after January 1, 2015 is not local-government debt.
It also states that the local government and SASAC act only as shareholders
with limited liability, while the issuer repays debt as an independent legal
person.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit. Formal no-government-financing language
coexists with continuing public transportation infrastructure investment,
government-authorized land development, legacy BT repayment, PPP participation,
government-related receivables, and project funds linked to municipal
transportation construction. The same issuer remains the relevant local
public-development vehicle after the formal compliance event.

## Caveat

This is a specialized municipal transportation SOE rather than a generic urban
construction platform. It is included because the source packet shows a
platform-like local financing and public-development role. The formal event is
based on prospectus and legal-opinion compliance language rather than an
independently collected platform-list transfer-out notice.
