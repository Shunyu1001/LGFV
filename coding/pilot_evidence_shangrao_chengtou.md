## Formal event

The prospectus and legal opinion state that the issuer does not undertake
government financing functions after January 1, 2015. The same packet states
that the issue will not increase government debt or hidden debt and will not be
directly repaid by fiscal funds.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because formal no-government-financing
language is paired with continuing land, infrastructure, and resettlement
functions inside the same issuer. A fiscal-substitution reading is plausible,
but the evidence does not show that the quasi-fiscal function disappeared.

## Caveat

The packet relies on 2026 issuance and legal-opinion disclosures rather than a
separately collected local platform-list exit notice.
