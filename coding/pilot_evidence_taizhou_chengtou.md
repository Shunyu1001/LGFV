## Coding Summary

Taizhou City Construction Investment is coded as nominal exit. The legal
opinion describes the issuer as moving from a government financing platform to
a city-industry investment operator, and the packet contains standard
no-hidden-debt and no-direct-fiscal-repayment language. Yet the same packet
shows continuing municipal infrastructure, shantytown and resettlement
construction, finance-bureau receivables, infrastructure project inventory,
fiscal subsidies, and delegated public-project functions.

The formal identity changes, but the issuer remains a public-development
vehicle. The case is therefore nominal exit rather than substantive exit.
