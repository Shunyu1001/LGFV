## Coding Summary

Tianjin Jinnan City Construction Investment is coded as nominal exit. The packet
contains no-hidden-debt and marketized-operation language, but it also shows
that the issuer remains the district vehicle for demonstration-town
construction, entrusted construction, resettlement housing, fiscal repayment,
and finance-bureau receivables. The issuer's public-development function
therefore persists after formal compliance language.

This case is kept at medium confidence because the legal opinion also reports
that the issuer belonged to the national government-debt audit platform list
and the original CBRC platform list. The current source packet documents
compliance and marketization rather than an independently collected transfer-out
notice.
