# Gaochun International Cittaslow Cultural Tourism Investment Group

Case ID: `sch_20260630_0142`

Company: 江苏高淳国际慢城文化旅游产业投资集团有限公司

Final label: `nominal_exit`

Confidence: `medium`

## Formal event

The 2026 short-term commercial paper prospectus and legal opinion contain the
standard post-2015 compliance language. The prospectus states that the issuer's
government-related receivables have engineering or operating backgrounds, that
the current issue will not increase local-government debt or hidden debt, that
the Gaochun state shareholder bears only limited liability, that the issuer is
responsible for repayment as an independent legal person, that the company does
not undertake government financing functions, and that new debt after January
1, 2015 is not local-government debt. The legal opinion repeats that the issue
will not add government debt or hidden debt, denies illegal guarantees,
government-credit-linked misleading publicity, land-reserve functions, PPP,
government investment fund, project repurchase, government purchase service,
and advance financing for government projects, and says fiscal funds will not
directly repay the bond.

## Continued function

The same source packet shows that the issuer continues to perform public
development functions. The legal opinion and audit report describe the company
as responsible for investment, financing, construction, and management of
cultural-tourism projects around Gucheng Lake, Gaochun Old Street, Yaxi
International Cittaslow, and Youzi Mountain, while also managing infrastructure,
cultural and sports facilities, tourist infrastructure projects, transport
facilities, and real-estate development. The prospectus identifies the issuer
as the investment and operating body for Gaochun tourism infrastructure and
states that major projects include Gucheng Lake ecological tourism resort, old
street preservation and redevelopment, Youzi Mountain National Forest Park, and
Yaxi International Cittaslow. The issuer's infrastructure construction is
implemented through entrusted-construction contracts with the Gaochun Cittaslow
management committee. The documents also report negative operating cash flow
linked to front-loaded project investment, large interest-bearing debt,
government-related receivables, government subsidies, and government payment
timing risk.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit. The issuer's disclosure package gives clean
formal compliance language, but the same issuer remains the public-development
vehicle for tourism infrastructure, entrusted construction, and project
financing in Gaochun. It relies on government subsidies, carries large
government-related receivables, and faces repayment risk tied to the timing and
capacity of the local project counterparty. Official legal separation therefore
does not amount to functional disappearance of the local public-development
role.

## Caveat

This is a district-level cultural-tourism and infrastructure platform under
Nanjing rather than a generic prefecture-level urban construction company. The
formal event is supported by prospectus and legal-opinion compliance language
rather than an independently collected administrative platform-list exit
notice.
