# Dezhou Deda Investment Holding

Case ID: `expand_sd_dezhou_deda`

Company: 德州德达投资控股集团有限公司

Final label: `nominal_exit`

Confidence: `high`

## Formal event

The legal opinion states that Dezhou CBRC approved the company's platform
loans for rectification into general-company loans in December 2016. It also
quotes a Dezhou government announcement stating that the company no longer
undertook local-government debt-financing functions. The prospectus states
that the company was not in the CBIRC government financing platform list as of
June 30, 2019 and does not undertake government financing functions after
January 1, 2015.

## Continued function

infrastructure construction, delegated construction, PPP-linked receivables,
government debt replacement, and fiscal-settlement mechanisms. The prospectus
explains how Dezhou fiscal debt-replacement funds were used in relation to
projects for which the issuer performed construction and later offset

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because a formal exit from platform-loan
management did not remove the issuer from infrastructure and land-development
functions linked to fiscal settlement and project receivables. A
fiscal-substitution reading is plausible, but the same issuer remains the
site of the core public-development function.

## Caveat

The direct formal event is supported by 2026 legal opinion and prospectus
disclosures rather than the original 2016 notice or government announcement.
Confidence is high because the formal event is quoted and the
continued-function evidence is detailed.
