Final label: `nominal_exit`

Confidence: `high`

## Formal event

The prospectus provides direct formal-exit evidence. It states that a 2011
Sichuan CBRC notice transferred the issuer out of the local government
financing platform customer list and placed it under rectified
general-company loan management. Later compliance language states that the
issuer does not undertake government financing functions after January 1,
2015.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because direct transfer-out evidence
coexists with continuing public-development functions inside the same issuer
group. Functional transfer remains a possible secondary reading because some
activities are carried through subsidiaries and compliant PPP or purchase
service arrangements, but the issuer group still houses the relevant function.

## Caveat

Direct transfer-out evidence comes from a later prospectus disclosure rather
than the original 2011 Sichuan CBRC notice. Confidence is high because formal
exit and continued-function evidence are both explicit.
