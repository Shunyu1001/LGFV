Final label: `nominal_exit`

## Formal event

The prospectus states that the issuer had been transferred out of the local
government financing platform list determined by the banking regulator. It also
reports that the issuer was included in the 2013 national government-debt audit
platform-company scope, with 1.2 billion yuan of borrowings counted as local
government debt at the end of June 2013, but that the corresponding balance was
zero by March 2026. The same compliance section states that the issuer does not
undertake government financing functions and that new debt after January 1,
2015 is not local-government debt.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit. The formal exit evidence is unusually direct:
the issuer reports that it had been transferred out of the banking-regulator
platform list and that borrowings previously counted as government debt had
fallen to zero. Yet the same issuer group continues to house infrastructure
construction, public housing investment and financing, government-linked BT
repurchase claims, government receivables, contract assets from BT projects,
and subsidy-dependent public-development activity. The institutional form
changed more clearly than the underlying local-development function.

## Caveat

The direct transfer-out evidence is a later prospectus disclosure rather than
the original banking-regulator notice. The exact transfer-out date has not been
independently collected. Confidence is high for the label because both the
formal event and continued-function evidence are explicit and repeated across
the source packet.
