# Jianan Investment Holding Group

Case ID: `sch_20260630_0027`

Company: 建安投资控股集团有限公司

Final label: `nominal_exit`

Confidence: `medium`

## Formal event

The prospectus and legal opinion use the standard post-2015 compliance formula:
the issuer states that bond proceeds will not be transferred to government or
fiscal departments, that the government will not repay the debt through fiscal
funds, that the government acts only as shareholder with limited liability, and
that the issuer does not undertake government financing functions. The same
language states that new debt after January 1, 2015 is not local-government
debt. The source packet also records that these claims were checked with the
Bozhou Finance Bureau.

## Continued function

The same source packet shows that the issuer continues to house major public
development functions. It reports land development and consolidation, agency
construction of government public infrastructure, and government purchase
service projects. The prospectus describes Bozhou Finance Bureau payment
mechanisms for entrusted construction projects, annual settlement documents,
government project repurchase or repayment arrangements, fiscal subsidies, and
large receivables from Bozhou Finance Bureau and other government entities.
The issuer is also described as the main implementation body for Bozhou
infrastructure construction and city development.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit. The official compliance language denies a
government financing role after 2015, but the same issuer continues to perform
land development, agency construction, government purchase service, fiscal
settlement, and subsidy-backed public-development functions. The institutional
form is formally cleaned up, while the public finance function remains inside
the issuer group.

## Caveat

The formal event is supported by prospectus and legal-opinion compliance
language rather than by an independently collected platform-list transfer-out
notice. Confidence is medium because the continued-function evidence is strong
and explicit, but the formal exit event is regulatory disclosure rather than a
separate government exit document.
