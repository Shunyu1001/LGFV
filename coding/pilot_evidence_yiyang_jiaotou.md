and transformed into a marketized self-operating entity. It also reports a
2019 city-level platform transformation plan under which the shareholder was
transferred from the Finance Bureau to SASAC.

## Coding rationale

The case is coded as nominal exit because formal transformation and SASAC
ownership coexist with continuing fiscal buyback and delegated infrastructure
construction. A functional-transfer reading remains plausible, but the current
evidence still attaches the public-project role to the same issuer.

## Caveat

The issuer is reported as outside national and CBIRC platform lists, but the
formal event is inferred from 2019 transformation disclosures rather than a
directly collected transfer-out notice.
